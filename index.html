The Wisconsin Fly Fishing Alliance is dedicated to promoting fly fishing and providing educational resources across various communities in Wisconsin. We emphasize empowering the youth of our state to explore and enjoy the extensive angling opportunities available. From the northern rivers to the Driftless Region in the south, Wisconsin offers a wealth of top-tier fly fishing experiences.

Building on our commitment to community and education, the Wisconsin Fly Fishing Alliance organizes numerous events throughout the year. These include workshops, competitions, and conservation projects, all designed to foster a deeper connection with nature and sustainable fishing practices. By engaging anglers of all ages, especially our youth, we aim to cultivate a lifelong passion for fly fishing and a commitment to preserving Wisconsin's beautiful waterways for future generations.
